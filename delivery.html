For My Clients

From Shoot Day
to Your Hands

Every gallery moves through the same careful process — here's exactly what happens after your session, and when you can expect each step.

The Process

Simple & Seamless

01

Sneak Peek

Within 24–48 hours of your shoot, I send a small preview of 5–10 favorite frames — so you're not left wondering how it went while the full edit is underway.

02

Editing in Progress

I personally cull and edit every image — no batch filters, no shortcuts. Most galleries are ready in 7–14 days; larger weddings and events may take slightly longer, and I'll keep you posted.

03

Private Client Gallery

Your finished gallery arrives as a password-protected link, viewable on any device. Galleries stay live for 90 days — download everything before then, or ask and I'll extend it.

04

Favorites & Album Picks

Mark your favorites directly inside the gallery. For wedding and portrait clients, this list becomes the starting point for any printed album or wall-art selection.

05

Download & Print

Every image comes in two versions — full-resolution files for printing, and web-optimized versions ready to share on social media. Download instantly, no waiting on a courier.

Good to Know

A Few Extra Touches

Highlight Reel
Wedding and event clients get a short 30–60 second video montage of stills, ready to share the same day as your gallery.
RAW Files
Unedited RAW files are available on request for brand and commercial shoots that need full post-production control.
Print & USB Keepsakes
Branded USB drives, printed albums, and framed prints are available as paid add-ons — ask about pricing when you book.
Reprints, Anytime
I keep your files safely archived for 12 months after delivery, so a reprint or an extra retouch later is never a hassle.
Refer a Friend
Loved your gallery? Share the word — friends and family you refer get 10% off their first booking.
Status Updates
If editing is going to take longer than usual — say, during wedding season — I'll message you proactively with a new timeline.
